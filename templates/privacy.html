Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

Effective Date: May 3, 2025

College Bound Tours (operated by ENGAM Solutions, LLC) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This document outlines our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

1. Who We Are

College Bound Tours is a service provided by ENGAM Solutions, LLC that organizes and facilitates group tours to colleges and universities for high school students and their guardians.

2. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

  • Name, email, phone number
  • User role (student, parent, admin, driver)
  • Emergency contact details
  • Student-parent linking data
  • User-provided photos or social media handles (optional)

b. Tour Registration and Participation Data

  • Tour sign-up history and preferences
  • Waitlist status and guest inclusion
  • Contact confirmation status
  • Consent forms and acknowledgments

c. Technical Data

  • IP address, browser, and device data
  • Login timestamps
  • Usage patterns for analytics

d. Payment and Financial Information

  • Payment details (via Stripe)
  • Transaction history

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Managing tour registrations and attendance
  • Sending reminders, alerts, and updates
  • Displaying personalized dashboards
  • Collecting and verifying parent-student relationships
  • Providing drivers with routing information
  • Ensuring compliance with school absence policies
  • Processing payments securely via Stripe
